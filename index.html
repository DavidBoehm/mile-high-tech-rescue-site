Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What areas do you serve?
We serve the entire Denver Metro area including Denver, Aurora, Lakewood, Littleton, Centennial, Highlands Ranch, and Boulder. Travel within 25 miles of downtown Denver is included in our rates.

Do you offer emergency services?
Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency support for critical issues like security breaches, complete network outages, or smart home lockouts. Emergency calls have an additional $150 fee but we prioritize getting you back up and running fast.

What smart home brands do you work with?
We work with all major brands including Ring, Nest, Philips Hue, Lutron, Ecobee, August, Yale, and many more. We're brand-agnostic—we recommend what works best for your needs and budget, not what gives us the best commission.

Do you offer warranties?
All our work comes with a 90-day warranty. If something we installed or configured stops working within 90 days, we'll fix it free of charge. Hardware warranties are handled through manufacturers, but we'll help you navigate those claims.

Can you work with existing equipment?
Absolutely! We'll work with what you have and integrate new equipment where needed. We'll tell you honestly if something should be replaced versus repaired. No pressure to buy new gear unless it actually makes sense.

Do I need to be home during the service?
For most services, yes—we'll need access and want to walk you through what we did. However, for businesses or repeat clients, we can arrange secure access. We'll always verify the work with you before considering it complete.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Venmo, PayPal, and checks. For business clients, we offer Net-30 terms with approved credit. Payment is due at time of service unless arranged otherwise.
